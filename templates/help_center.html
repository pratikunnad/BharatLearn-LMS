Help Center

Find answers to common questions, learn how BharatLearn works, and get support whenever you need it.

Getting Started

What is BharatLearn?

BharatLearn is a learning platform that organizes and curates courses, tutorials, and reference material from multiple trusted sources into one easy-to-use dashboard.

Do you create your own courses?

BharatLearn primarily groups and organizes educational content from different platforms. Course ownership and rights belong to their respective creators.

Is BharatLearn free?

Many features are free. Some courses or external platforms may require payment, which is clearly mentioned before access.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Click on “Get Started” or “Register” and fill in your basic information to create an account.

Why is my profile image or data not updating?

If you recently edited your profile, please refresh the page. Users are responsible for keeping their account information accurate and up-to-date. If the issue continues, log out and log back in.

Courses & Reference Books

I cannot see a PDF or book cover

This usually happens if the file path is missing or the file was not uploaded correctly. Please contact support if the issue persists.

Can I download reference books?

Most reference books are provided in read-only mode to respect copyright and licensing rules.

Data & Privacy

How does BharatLearn handle my data?

BharatLearn collects only the minimum information required to create and manage user accounts, provide access to learning resources, and respond to support requests.

Do you share my data with third parties?

We do not sell or rent personal data. Limited data may be shared only when required to provide platform functionality or comply with legal obligations.

Where can I learn more?

For complete details on data collection, usage, storage, and user rights, please review our Privacy Policy.
